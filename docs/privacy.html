Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2025

Welcome to Focus Three. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

TL;DR: We collect minimal data (only what's needed for the app to work). Your tasks are stored locally on your device. When you sign in, we sync them to the cloud. You can export or delete your data anytime.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in, we collect:

  • Email address - Provided by Google or Apple when you sign in with OAuth
  • User ID - A unique identifier assigned by our authentication provider
  • Authentication tokens - Used to keep you signed in securely

1.2 Task Data

To provide the core functionality of the app, we store:

  • Task titles - The text you enter for each task
  • Task size - Small, Medium, or Big
  • Completion status - Whether a task is active or completed
  • Timestamps - When tasks were created and completed
  • Task history - Your completed tasks timeline

1.3 App Preferences

  • Theme selection - Which color theme you've chosen
  • Language preference - Your selected language (English, Finnish, etc.)

1.4 Subscription Information

When you subscribe:

  • Subscription status - Managed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store
  • Subscription validation - Handled by RevenueCat (our payment processor)
  • We do NOT store your payment information - this is handled entirely by Apple/Google

1.5 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, phone number, or physical address
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics or tracking pixels
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

  • Local Storage - Your tasks are stored locally on your device using SQLite database
  • Cloud Sync - When you sign in, your tasks sync to our Supabase cloud database so they're available on all your devices
  • Offline-First - The app works fully offline; cloud sync is supplementary

2.2 Authentication

  • We use Supabase Authentication for secure sign-in
  • OAuth providers (Google, Apple) handle the actual authentication
  • We use your email only to identify your account

2.3 Subscription Management

  • We use RevenueCat to validate your subscription status
  • This ensures you have access to the app
  • RevenueCat does not receive your task data

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • Local Device - Primary storage in SQLite database on your device
  • Supabase Cloud - When signed in, data synced to Supabase (hosted on AWS)
  • RevenueCat - Subscription status only (no task data)

3.2 Security Measures

  • All data transmitted to cloud servers uses HTTPS encryption
  • Supabase uses Row Level Security (RLS) - you can only access your own data
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely using platform-specific secure storage
  • We never store passwords (OAuth providers handle authentication)

3.3 Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as you use the app
  • If you sign out, data remains on your device but stops syncing
  • If you delete your account, all cloud data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Local data is deleted when you uninstall the app

4. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

Under GDPR and similar privacy laws, you have the following rights:

4.1 Right to Access

  • You can export all your data as JSON or CSV from the Settings screen
  • This includes all tasks, history, and preferences

4.2 Right to Delete

  • You can delete individual tasks within the app
  • You can sign out to stop cloud sync (keeps local data)
  • To delete all cloud data, contact us and we'll remove it within 30 days

4.3 Right to Portability

  • Export your data in standard formats (JSON, CSV)
  • Take your data to another app if you choose

4.4 Right to Rectification

  • You can edit or update your tasks at any time
  • Changes sync automatically if signed in

5. Third-Party Services

Focus Three uses the following third-party services:

5.1 Supabase (Database and Authentication)

  • Purpose: Cloud database and user authentication
  • Data shared: Email, user ID, tasks, preferences
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

5.2 RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

  • Purpose: Validate App Store and Play Store subscriptions
  • Data shared: User ID, subscription status
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

5.3 Google / Apple (OAuth Authentication)

  • Purpose: Secure sign-in
  • Data shared: Email address (only with your consent)
  • Google Privacy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple Privacy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

6. Children's Privacy

Focus Three is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. For significant changes, we'll notify you via email or an in-app message.

8. International Users

Focus Three is available worldwide. If you use the app from outside the European Union, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries with different privacy laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your rights, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@ttvanhanen.fi
  • Website: https://focusthree.ttvanhanen.fi

We'll respond to your request within 30 days.
